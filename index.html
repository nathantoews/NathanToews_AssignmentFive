    GOAL

    At Ranger we want to create an social experience where we are creating a network of user based animal information to help create a community based around animal awareness.

    Animal patterns, habits, migration patterns and locations are all information that we want to collect to both help environmentalists and nature lovers looking to seek the best animal watching experience.

    This data we hope will further help the preservation and understanding of the creatures we share the world with.
